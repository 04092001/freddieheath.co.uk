Digital Media

This unit introduces students to a range of practice-based elements in digital media output which requires them to produce an artefact and reflect on the artefact drawing on theoretical perspectives developed in the unit and across the course at large. It introduces theories, processes, technologies and materials used in digital media production. Future technologies will be covered and practical workshops will be offered to introduce students to industry standard software used to generate online solutions.
